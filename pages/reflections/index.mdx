I don't exist / But in your imagination<br/>
You don't exist / But in mine<br/>
Nowhere else / Do we exist<br/>
But in each other's minds

The moon is but the sun's / Reflection<br/>
Your eyes / Mine<br/>
I don't exist / I'm a figment of your imagination<br/>
You don't exist but in mine
